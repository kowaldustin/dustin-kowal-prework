About Me

I am Dustin Kowal and I just completed my B.S. in Computer Information Systems. I enjoy programming because it allows me to tap into my creative side and design whatever I want from scratch. One of my earliest desires was to become an artist but I soon realized that my artistic skills are not aligned with that dream. However through software engineering and design I am still able to achieve that original desire.

Now that my time is no longer filled with homework and school studies I rely on my two main hobbies to survive the quarantine situation. These are music and appreciation of nature. One of my favorite pass times, especially in Duluth, is finding a new hiking trail or interesting spot whether it's a waterfall, old foundations, or some other pituresque spot.

When the weather is not cooperative I turn to my other hobby, playing piano or guitar. Recently I've had a lot of fun attempting to recreate classic rock songs like Hold Me Now without referencing any other music, essentially seeing how close I can get to the original sound and feel of the song.
